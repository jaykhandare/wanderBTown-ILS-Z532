Anatolia

About Anatolia:

Nestled squarely among the best restaurants that compose Bloomington’s incredibly diverse Fourth Street, Anatolia takes the crown because of the outstanding quality of the restaurant’s food.

Featuring a menu of classic Mediterranean and Turkish dishes, from kebabs to stuffed eggplant and baked dolma, Turkish stuffed grape leaves, Anatolia is authentic Turkish fare right here in Bloomington.

Whether you’re looking for a nostalgic taste from home or a meal that breaks out of the everyday humdrum, Anatolia is the spot to get the best ethnic food. in town.
